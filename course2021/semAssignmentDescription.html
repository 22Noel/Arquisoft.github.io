Introduction

The goal is this assignment is that small teams of 2-4 students will analyze some aspect of software architecture. The list of subjects is and the teams are proposed by the teachers.

The students will prepare a short presentation of 15 minutes where they will present those subjects from the point of view of software architecture.

Those presentations will be followed by a 10 minutes session of questions.
